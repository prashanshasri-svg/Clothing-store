The Manifesto

Consciously crafted for the urban landscape.

UrbanThread was born from a simple observation: modern streetwear often forces a choice between aesthetic edge and ethical substance. We believed there had to be a way to create high-street minimalism without compromising on environmental responsibility.

Founded in 2022 in a small studio in London, we started by reimagining the essential hoodie. We sourced organic, heavy-loopback cotton and spent months refining the pattern to achieve the perfect drop-shoulder drape. That single piece set the foundation for our entire design philosophy.

Design Language

Our aesthetic is rooted in the intersection of high-fashion editorial and raw urban utility. We utilize a restricted color palette, allowing the quality of the fabrics and the precision of the cut to stand as the focal point. We believe in generous whitespace, both in our digital presence and in our physical silhouettes.

Sustainability as Standard

"Sustainable" isn't a marketing buzzword for us; it's the baseline requirement for operation. We use 100% GOTS-certified organic cotton, recycled synthetic fibers, and biodegradable packaging. Our supply chain is entirely transparent, partnering only with factories that guarantee living wages and safe working conditions.

We design pieces meant to be worn heavily, washed repeatedly, and kept for years. This is our commitment to slowing down the fashion cycle.
